Jennifer Lawrence Nude Photos Leaked: &quot;Flagrant Violation of Privacy&quot;

No one’s laughing now. Oscar winner Jennifer Lawrence has fallen victim to a nude photo leak, the American Hustle star’s rep confirms to Us Weekly. Lawrence’s phone was hacked, leading to nude selfies of the star surfacing around the Internet.

“This is a flagrant violation of privacy,” Lawrence’s spokesperson tells Us in a statement. “The authorities have been contacted and will prosecute anyone who posts the stolen photos of Jennifer Lawrence.”

According to Buzzfeed, the Hunger Games actress, 24, is one of many A-listers who has been hacked. Others on the list include “Problem” singer Ariana Grande, Nickelodeon star Victoria Justice, supermodel Kate Upton, and actress Kirsten Dunst.

The other female stars have not confirmed the authenticity of their alleged photos. The photos originally were posted on the /b/ thread on 4chan and have not been taken down despite Lawrence’s spokesperson’s statement. The site claims to also have nude photos of newlywed Gabrielle Union, pregnant Hayden Panettiere, former Disney star Hilary Duff, The Big Bang Theory‘s Kaley Cuoco, Lea Michele, Mary-Kate Olsen, Selena Gomez, and many more.
