About UBS Group AG

UBS Group AG is a multinational investment bank and financial services company headquartered in Switzerland. With co-headquarters in the cities of Zürich and Basel, UBS Group AG has established itself as the largest Swiss banking institution and one of the largest private banks in the world.

With a rich history dating back to 1862, UBS Group AG has built a strong reputation for its expertise in wealth management, investment banking, and asset management.
