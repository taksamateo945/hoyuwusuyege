It Will Rain Lyrics

It Will Rain is a soulful ballad by the acclaimed American singer-songwriter Bruno Mars.

Released in 2011 as a lead single from the soundtrack of the movie “The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 1,” the song quickly became a commercial success, captivating audiences with its heartfelt lyrics and Mars’ powerful vocals
