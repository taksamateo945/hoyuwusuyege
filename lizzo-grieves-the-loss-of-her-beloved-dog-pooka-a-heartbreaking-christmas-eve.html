Lizzo Grieves the Loss of Her Beloved Dog, Pooka: A Heartbreaking Christmas Eve

In a grievous turn of events, Grammy award-winning artist Lizzo mourns the loss of her cherished dog, Pooka. The singer’s furry companion passed away on Christmas Eve, casting a somber shadow over her holiday season.

A Deep Bond Broken

Lizzo, known for her vibrant personality and powerful music, shared her heartbreak publicly with fans, expressing that she was “not okay” following the loss of Pooka. The bond between the singer and her pet was evident in her candid social media posts, which often featured Pooka.
