How Old is Marlon Hampton?

Marlo Hampton is 47 years old. She was born the 7th of February 1976 in America’s Sunshine State, precisely in the part of Florida known as Clearwater. Being born in the United States makes Marlo a citizen of America, however, her physical appearance is a testament to her Afro-American origin.

Nothing has been said about her father, but her mum is said to be a woman simply called Emma. Marlo is not an only child, she grew up alongside a sister called Crystal and a brother who was known as Curtis Williams Jr. Sadly, Curtis battled a traumatic brain injury that led to his death at a very young age.

Throughout her life, Marlo’s mum, Emma, battled an addiction to heroin and it goes without saying that it had a dire effect on her parenting capability. It got even worse when you consider the fact that her husband was not even present to cover up for her shortcomings. When their Emma’s addiction got to an alarming stage, her children had to be taken into foster care and according to Marlo Hampton, she was barely nine at that time.

After being moved from one foster home to the other, Marlo finally put down roots with the Hopkins family who went ahead to adopt her. The Hopkins were resident in St. Petersburg, Florida, and that was where she completed her growing-up years. Talking about her adoptive parents, Marlo would describe how they took her in when her world was crumbling and offered her unconditional love.

Marlo’s foster mum was called Arrie and she lived up to 103 years. It was a few days after her 103rd birthday that she passed on.

She Started her Career Journey in the fashion Industry

As earlier mentioned, Marlo Hampton’s first port of call was the fashion industry. After she was through with her academic endeavors, she decided to launch a business in the fashion industry by floating a high-end boutique known as Red Carpet Boutique.

It’s important to note that she left her home state, Florida, after completing her studies and went in search of greener pastures in Atlanta, Georgia, where she cited her fashion boutique. The boutique hit the ground running in 2008 and before long, it acquired a robust clientele base, catering to the fashion needs of the crème de la crème of Atlanta’s elite community.

Customers who patronize Marlo Hampton’s boutique are offered her own sense of fashion style. Thanks to Marlo’s passion for haute couture, she was able to penetrate the rich and powerful and with her drive and perseverance, she left a lasting impression on them.

Hampton took her sublime fashion sense to the set of The Real Housewives of Atlanta reality show. She fielded fans in question and answer sessions, teaching them how she came about her flair and knack for style, and it was most beneficial for people who wished to follow suit.

Throughout her career in the fashion industry, Marlo has done admirably well in educating female folks about fashion tips. We must also acknowledge the Art of Style Tour, which is her multi-city fashion seminar targeted at inspiring female stylists and designers.

Marlo has made her mark in the fashion industry, serving at the 2016 International Fashion Week as a Style Ambassador. The exact amount that she rakes in from her fashion business has never been disclosed but it is quite huge and contributes a great deal to her bank balance.

Marlo Hampton’s Net Worth has Also Accumulated From Reality TV
