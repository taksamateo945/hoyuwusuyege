When Will All American Season 4 Premiere on The CW?

There are only a few ticks left on the clock when it comes to the third season of All American. The season finale of the popular Daniel Ezra-led sports drama airs tonight on The CW, but there’s plenty more on the way. Earlier this year, The CW not only renewed All American for a fourth season, but the network also announced an All American spinoff, All American: Homecoming, that’s scheduled to premiere in 2022. Why kick the extra point when you can go for two, am I right?

The Season 3 finale airs tonight, but if you can’t watch the final episode live, the third season of All American will make its Netflix debut on Tuesday, July 27. The Season 3 finale will also be available to stream on The CW website and CW app beginning Tuesday, July 20.

With Season 3 almost in the books, many fans are wondering about the fourth season of All American. When will All American Season 4 premiere on The CW? Here’s everything you need to know.

WHAT TIME IS THE ALL AMERICAN SEASON 3 FINALE ON TONIGHT?

The season finale of All American airs tonight (July 19) from 8:00-9:00 p.m. ET on The CW. If you don’t have cable, you can watch All American live with an active subscription to Hulu + Live TV, YouTube TV, or AT&T TV NOW. All of the aforementioned services offer The CW in their TV channel bundles.

The All American Season 3 finale will also be available for next-day streaming (for free!) on both The CW website and CW app.

HAS ALL AMERICAN BEEN RENEWED FOR SEASON 4?

Yes! Earlier this year, The CW renewed All American for a fourth season.

WHEN WILL ALL AMERICAN SEASON 4 PREMIERE ON THE CW?

Back in June, The CW released their forthcoming fall schedule. All American Season 4 premieres Monday, October 25, 2021 on The CW.

WHEN WILL ALL AMERICAN SEASON 3 BE ON NETFLIX?

The third season of All American premieres Tuesday, July 27 on Netflix!
