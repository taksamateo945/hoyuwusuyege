Kim Kardashian Pets - Celebrity Pets

Rocky (Dog)

Breed: Boxer | Adopted 2010

When Kim was dating Reggie Bush, Rocky was a boxer puppy they got together in 2010 and named after the classic boxing movie. She was quoted as saying “Rocky is most like me, his mommy. He’s really cool and calm and goes with the flow”. They apparently had split custody after their breakup, but Rocky hasn’t been seen with Kim since.

Princess (Dog)

Breed: Chihuahua

In 2009, Princess was found by Kim outside of a nail salon and adopted by her. She soon found out Princess had health problems that needed surgery and constant care. Due to Kim’s busy schedule, she wasn’t able to keep her, but luckily her vet’s assistant took Princess in.

Mercy (Cat)

Breed: Fluffy White Cat | Adopted 2012

Mercy the cat was a gift from Kanye West to Kim in 2012, which Kim named after a song Kanye released at that time. Unfortunately Kim found out she was allergic to Mercy, so she was given to her sister Khloe’s assistant who cat had recently passed away. Soon after, Mercy was found unconscious and rushed to an animal hospital.

Goldfish (Fish)

Breed: Goldfish

While Kim’s daughter North has been asking for a puppy, Kim has started her off with five goldfish. Their names are Little Mermaid, Zuma, Chase, Sky and Shopkins.

Bella (Dog)

Breed: Maltese

Bella is a Maltese that had been with the Kardashians for years, but now living with family friend Pastor Brad and his wife. No one is sure why Bella went to go live with Pastor Brad, and the mystery surrounding the Jenner-Kardashian pets seems to grow. We hope you’re happy with your new family, Bella!

Simba (Dog)

Breed: Pomeranian

She has a Pomeranian named Simba.

Sushi and Sake (Dog)

Breed: Pomeranian

Kim Kardashian has two Pomeranians with Japanese cuisine-themed names: The beige Pom is Sushi and the white Pom is Sake.

Speed (Reptile)

Breed: Bearded Dragon | Adopted Mar 2021

Kim Kardashian’s daughter North West has a Bearded Dragon lizard named Speed. Kim publicly revealed the lizard in March 2021 but had got it several months before.

Soba (Soy Sauce) (Dog)

Breed: Pomeranian | Adopted Sep 2019

When Kim Kardashian got her white Pomeranian girl Sake in September 2019, she also got a black Pom boy that she named Soba (with Soy Sauce being the second choice for a name). Soba the black Pom hasn’t been seen since or mentioned by Kim since, with only Sake and her beige Pom Sushi appearing on her Instagram. It’s assumed Soba was rehomed to someone outside her direct family as the pup hasn’t appeared on any of her sisters’ social media accounts.

Dolce (Dog)

Breed: Chihuahua | Adopted 2005

Dolce the Chihuahua was a family pet of the Kardashians and was seen on the early seasons of Keeping Up with the Kardashians. Dolce joined the Kardashian family around 2005, along with their Labrador Gabbana. Dolce went missing in 2012 and it was assumed a coyote killed him. Dolce is seen here with Kylie.

Gabbana (Dog)

Breed: Labrador | Adopted 2005

The Kardashian family got Gabbana in 2005, along with their late chihuahua Dolce. Gabbana was one of the longest-running Kardashian family pets. After her parent’s divorce, Khloe took Gabbana in. Gabbana unfortunately passed away in 2018. After her passing, Kris Jenner attempted to give Khloe three identical new puppies, but Khloe found the pups alternative homes. Apparently, no new puppies can fill Gabbana’s place in the Kardashian home. RIP Gabbana!

14 Friesian Horses (Horse)

Breed: Friesian | Adopted Jul 2019

In July 2020, Kim Kardashian tweeted that Kanye West and her added 14 Friesian horses to their Wyoming ranch in 2019. Note Friesian horses can cost between $7,000 to $600,000 each, making 14 quite the invoice.

Friesian horses are a ancient horse breed originating from Friesland, a province in the northern Netherlands. Known for their striking appearance, these horses are characterized by a solid, muscular build, a thick mane and tail, and a shiny black coat. What makes Friesians truly special is their graceful and powerful movement, which, along with their elegant and regal appearance, makes them highly sought after for dressage, show driving, and as carriage horses.

Kim Kardashian

Birthday

October 21, 1980 (43)

Kim Kardashian is an American reality TV star, socialite, entrepreneur, social media influencer, entrepreneur, and model. She first started appearing in the media as a friend of Paris Hilton and then received wide public attention when her sex tape was leaked in 2007. This was followed up with the premier of the long-running reality TV series Keeping Up with the Kardashians. The show features her now-famous family with sisters Kourtney Kardashian and Khloe Kardashian, mother Kris Jenner, niece Penelope Scotland Disick, and half-sisters Kendall Jenner and Kylie Jenner. She is also known for her marriage to Kanye West, who she had four kids with named North, Saint, Psalm, and Chicago. Oh yeah, she was also married to Kris Humphries for 72 days in 2011.

From reportedly being the highest-paid reality TV star and through a range of business ventures, she is worth an estimated $150 million. This includes her own fashion and beauty brands, often in collaboration with her sisters. She also works as an ambassador for many brands over the years and has published books and mobile apps.
