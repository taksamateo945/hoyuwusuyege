Jacky Oh Plastic Surgeon speaks for the first time

In a previous report by Us, it was announced on June 1 that Ms. Jacky Oh had tragically passed away.

Prior to her untimely death, she had shared videos of her time in Miami undergoing a “mommy makeover” cosmetic surgery.

After her passing, all photographs related to her procedure were removed from the doctor’s office.

Jacky Oh Plastic Surgeon speaks

Today, on June 16, Jacky Oh Plastic Surgeon Dr. Zach Okhah broke his silence for the first time since the tragedy involving Jacky Oh.

In an Instagram post, he addressed his past, current, and prospective patients, affirming that PH-1 Miami is fully dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care.

Dr. Okhah emphasized that all aesthetic procedures are conducted in a hygienic and safe environment, adhering to universally recognized medical standards.

He expressed his unwavering commitment to advancing techniques in the field of plastic surgery and highlighted the importance of thoroughly vetting, preparing and treating each patient based on their individual medical history before any surgery.

The doctor’s ultimate goal is to help patients achieve optimal results through the safest and most medically appropriate means possible.

Dr. Okhah chose not to mention Jacky Oh’s passing and disabled the comment section on his posts.

He shared his message on his Instagram Story, followed by another upload showcasing a “vaser lipo 360 [and] tummy tuck],” accompanied by the song “Forgot About Dre” by Dr. Dre and Eminem.

The 33-year-old Jacky Oh is survived by her three children, Nova (6), Nala (2), and Prince (10 months), whom she shared with her long-term partner, DC Young Fly.

During her Atlanta funeral service on Saturday, June 10, DC spoke about her passing, expressing the strength she derived from prayer and her unwavering faith.

He reassured everyone that he and their children were coping, guided by their spirituality rather than worldly desires.
