Know About Nick Chubb; Stats, Injury, College, High School, Dating

Among the rising NFL young stars, Nick Chubb is another name that no one should miss out on. The Cleveland Browns’ running back made his national league debut in 2018, after a handy college career with Georgia Bulldogs football team.

Nick Chubb found his interest in American football as both his father and uncle played the sport back in time. With that, the young player aims much to accomplish in this popular sport.

Nick Chubb Bio: Age, Siblings, Height

The 23-years-of-age player was born as Nicholas Jamaal Chubb on 27 December 1995 in Cedartown, Georgia, the United States. He is among the three children of his father, Henry Chubb. Nick shares a sibling bond with his brother, Zach Chubb and sister Neidra Chubb.

Nick Chubb with his siblings on 24 June 2019 (Picture: Nick’s Instagram)

Standing tall at a height of 5 feet and 11 inches (1.80 m), Nick attended Cedartown High School in his hometown. There, he was a two-sport star for the Bulldogs’ football and track teams. Following that, he attended the University of Georgia and enjoyed a four-season career with the Georgia Bulldogs football team.

Career Stats

Courtesy of his excellent college scores, the Cleveland Browns picked him in the second round of the 2018 NFL Draft. Eventually, he penned a four-year rookie deal worth $7.4 million with the team. Nick’s contract comes with a $3.4 million signing bonus.

Nick ringed his NFL debut in a season-opening game against the Pittsburgh Steelers where he recorded three carries for 21 yards. Later on 19 October 2018, he was named the full-time starter by the Browns. Following that, he broke off a 92-yard rushing touchdown to help his side secure a 28-16 victory over the Atlanta Falcons on 11 November 2018.

Catch Nick Chubb’s NFL career stats (Picture: Pro Football Reference)

In Week 2 of the 2019 season, Nick rushed 18 times for 62 yards and recorded his first touchdown of the season in the 23-3 win over the New York Jets.

Is Nick Chubb Dating?

Well, the NFL star was in a relationship before. He dated a beautiful girl named Laci Shaw for almost five years. The couple called quits to the relationship in 2018. Since then, Nick is single and completely focused on his professional career.

Some Interesting Facts About Nick Chubb

  • Nick is named for his great-grandfather who, along with seven brothers, founded Chubbtown, Georgia, a group of free blacks who endured through the American Civil War.
  • His father played at Valdosta State and his uncle, Aaron, was a linebacker for Georgia.
  • Nick had to miss most part of the 2015 college season due to a grotesque knee injury.
