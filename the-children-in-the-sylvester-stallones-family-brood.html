The Children in the Sylvester Stallones Family Brood

The Children in the Sylvester Stallone’s Family Brood

Sylvester Stallone is one of the few oldies but goodies in Hollywood.

Just a few people started their walk in fame years ago and are still on the journey.

Sylvester Stallone at 72 is the definition of Career goals. His acting career has been on for about 36 years now and he has been a recipient of top awards, nominations and recognition.

Date of Birth: July 6, 1946

Zodiac Sign: Cancer

You may also like: Sylvester Stallone’s parents and siblings

If you are one of those who have seen Stallone’s movies, you would know that he is a lover of action. Stallone created a niche for himself in the Action genre and in all his appearances he has delivered more than hundred percent. One worth noting is the Rocky series from 1976 to 2015 where his character was the movie title and protagonist. The latest movie in the Rocky series is Creed (2015) where he won his first Golden Globe award for Best Supporting Actor.

Sylvester with Sasha Czack

In Sylvester’s seventy years on earth, he has been through three marriages; he had kids with two; Sasha Czack (married from 1974 to 1985) and Jennifer Flavin (married since 1997). Let’s take a look at his children’s lives.

Sylvester with Jennife Flavin

Sage Moonblood Stallone (son, born to Sylvester Stallone and Sasha Czack)

Date of Birth: May 5, 1976

Date of Death: July 13, 2012 (aged 36)

Zodiac Sign: Taurus

As the first child and first son to Stallone, he went head on to pursue his father’s career in acting. Sage’s debut movie appearance was in the fifth Rocky series as the Robert Balboa, Jr. He was married to actress Starlin Wright but their marriage was cancelled after about a year. He was known as a horror film distributor.

Sadly in 2012, Sage Stallone was found dead in his Los Angeles at the age of 36. About a month after his death, the Los Angeles County Coroner’s Office mentioned that Sage died of a heart attack. Sage’s death shook his family as they all loved him and referred to him as the centre of their world. Who know how far he would have gone in the movie industry if death did not take him away.

Sylvester Stallone children

Seargeoh Stallone (son, born to Sylvester Stallone and Sasha Czack)

Year of Birth: 1979

Seargeoh is the handsome second son and the silent Stallone. He lives a very private life so much is not known about him. We think he couldn’t handle the hustle of his father’s celebrity status as he was diagnosed with autism at the young age of 3. We don’t know how much therapy Seargeoh has gotten but he has some experience in child acting.

Sophia Stallone (daughter, born to Sylvester Stallone and Jennifer Flavin)

Date of Birth: August 27, 1996

Zodiac Sign: Virgo

She is one of the three pretty girls in the Sly family brood. Of late they have become regular faces on social media. Sophia the first girl and is currently a student at the University of Southern California. The same year when Sage sadly died, Sophia had to go in for a heart surgery to correct a congenital valve defect.

Her mother Jennifer Flavin says Sophia resembles her father the most. I don’t think you should also be surprised to know that Sophia is the love of his life. We have seen severally how fathers literally adore their first daughters. Stallone has proven to be just like every other man in this regard. Who can fault him when they both think alike and have very similar gestures?

The first female Stallone is a lover of books and movies. In fact, she has read nearly all of Shakespeare’s books.

Sistene Stallone (daughter, born to Sylvester Stallone and Jennifer Flavin)

Date of Birth: June 27, 1998

Zodiac Sign: Cancer

Sophia already took after her father so when Sistene came on board she interestingly chose another path to follow. Sistene is the middle child of Jennifer Flavin and has decided to follow her mother’s path. She sees herself as a terrible actress and is an aspiring model and has been called by Vogue “a fresh face to watch”.

As much as Sistene did not take after her father, she needed her father’s fame in her rise to prominence. So many people know her because she is a Stallone. Sistene is glad for this opportunity but she hopes to create a name for herself soonest.

Scarlet Stallone (daughter, born to Sylvester Stallone and Jennifer Flavin)

Date of Birth: May 25, 2002

Zodiac Sign: Gemini

Oh Goodness! This was the first thing I said when I saw a picture of Sylvester Stallone and his last child. Scarlet is the female version of what Sylvester would have been as a young child. The Stallone baby shares a striking resemblance with her celebrity dad.

One time her father shared a video of her on Instagram winning in a track race. This goes to show that Scarlet did not just inherit his looks; she also inherited her father’s athletic abilities.
