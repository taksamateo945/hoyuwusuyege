Cardi B Was Twerking on Grandpa Pickles: The Rappers Wild Birthday Party for Her Daughter

The Rapper’s Wild Birthday Party for Her Daughter

Cardi B and Offset threw a lavish birthday bash for their daughter Kulture, who turned five on July 10.

The party was held at the American Dream Mall in New Jersey, where Kulture and her friends enjoyed a princess-themed celebration.

Kulture looked adorable in a pink leotard and tutu, playing a pink piano.

Cardi B shared some cute photos of her daughter on Instagram, gushing over how much she loves her.

Cardi B Was Twerking on Grandpa Pickles

But Cardi B also had some fun of her own at the party.

She spotted a statue of Grandpa Lou Pickles, the grandfather from the classic cartoon “Rugrats.”

Cardi B decided to spice things up by twerking on the statue while rapping along to her new song with FendiDa Rappa “Point Me 2.”

She slapped her booty and demanded money from Grandpa Lou, making us laugh with her hilarious antics.
