Who Is Avi Gupta? His Wikipedia, Jeopardy, Family and Facts

Avi Gupta is one of the semifinalists in the ongoing Jeopardy! High School Reunion Tournament. He secured a semifinal berth after winning the quarterfinal round on Tuesday, 21 February 2023.

What is Avi Gupta job? Learn about Avi Gupta Wikipedia, age, parents, Jeopardy, ethnicity, college, high school, bio, and some interesting facts.

Avi Gupta is the semifinalist of the 2023 Jeopardy! High School Reunion Tournament. He bagged the winning total of $21,300 after coming out victorious in the quarterfinal round on Tuesday, 21 February 2023.

Avi Gupta Wiki and Family; Age, Parents, Ethnicity

The Jeopardy! champ was born in 2002 in Portland, Oregon, United States. Avi is now 21 years of age. With American nationality, he has mixed ethnicity.

Avi was raised by his Indian parents. His mother, Nandita Gupta, and his father both are cardiologists by profession. His family members are super excited to see him return to Jeopardy! and secure a semifinal berth.

Talking about his education, Avi graduated from the Catlin Gabel School in 2019. Afterward, he earned his Bachelor of Science degree at Columbia University in the city of New York in 2020.

To the recent, Avi is studying M.S. Computer Science at Stanford University since May 2020.

What Is Avi Gupta Job? His Jeopardy! Run

Avi serves as a founder and executive director at Project32 since November 2016. Besides, he is popular as a contestant on the first-ever Jeopardy! High School Reunion Tournament.

The tournament featuring 27 high school graduates began airing on 20 February 2023. Avi appeared in Tuesday’s quarterfinal round on 21 February where he managed to defeat his challengers.

Avi won against a senior at Northwestern University, Jack Izzo, and a senior at the University of Texas at Austin, Alison Purcell. With the win, he booked a place in the semifinals of the competition and also added $21,300 to his winning purse.

Now, Avi will compete in the semifinals to inch closer to a grand winning prize of $100,000.

Avi Gupta Dating Status; Who Is His Girlfriend?

Avi seems single and not dating anyone at the moment. There is no news regarding his past or present girlfriend on the internet.

The 21-year-old seems solely focused on his college education as of now.

Some Interesting Facts About Avi Gupta

  • Avi previously won the 2019 Jeopardy! Teen Tournament and took home a cash prize of $100,000.
  • Avi was also a legislative intern at United States Senate where he served from January 2022 to March 2022.
  • Since 2021, he is a constitutional law center undergraduate fellow at Stanford Law School.
